After expressions of necessity or emotion — il faut que, vouloir que, bien que — the following verb must be in the subjunctive. Think of it as grammar's emotional register.

You chose partir (infinitive) — but after il faut que, French requires the subjunctive. The subjunctive of partir for third-person singular is parte. Notice: it often looks like the present tense minus the final consonant.
