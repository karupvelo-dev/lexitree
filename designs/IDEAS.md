## Unexplored

**French-native metaphors**
- Arrondissement map — hand-illustrated vintage Paris map. Words = address pins. Fog of war lifts as you learn. Levels = arrondissements.
- Notre-Dame reconstruction — stone by stone. Sections = levels. Emotionally resonant post-2019.
- Versailles garden — geometric formal beds. Words = flowers. Mastered = full bloom.
- Louvre gallery — empty frames fill with paintings as words are mastered.
- French café chalkboard — chalk menu. Words written/erased as mastery changes.

**Spatial / structural**
- Typographic constellation — words ARE the stars. Size + opacity = mastery. No metaphor layer.
- Progress shelf / étagère — books on a shelf. Spines fill with colour as mastered.
- Stained glass window — Gothic rose window. Fragments fill with colour by level.
- Archaeological dig grid — words as buried artefacts. Excavate upward from A1.
- Atelier wall — studio cork board. Words pinned as sketches → finished paintings.

**Data-first**
- Periodic table — POS = element group. Level = period row. Highest density (all 260 words on screen).
- Semantic web / knowledge graph — force-directed. Words connected by meaning. Mastered = glowing node. V3.
- Temperature / recency map — mastery state + time decay. Warm this week, cooling after 10 days. (Fadell)
- Priority queue — words ranked by least-seen + lowest mastery. One-tap drill button. (Knapp)
- Pure opacity float — no metaphor. Words at varying opacity on white. (Ive)
